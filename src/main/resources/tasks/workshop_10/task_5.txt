Task#5

- Generate an ArrayList of first 12 Fibonacci Numbers (formulas: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fibonacci_number)
- Generate the second ArrayList of 100 random integers in range from 0 to 150
- Count how much Fibonacci numbers contains the second ArrayList: in sum and per separate number

E.g.
random generated list:
[1, 94, 32, 13, 44, 1, 145]

Console output:
Found 2 Fibonacci numbers:
1 - found 2 times
13 - found 1 time
